No, I'm not a Human

By Ghaidaa Benrekia


Amidist a solar apocalypse that makes days fatal, we must act as a homeowner deciding which nightly visitors to shelter.


The goal is to indentify VISITORS, skinwalking doppelgangers, from humans using specific clues to avoid violent encounters. Some of these "clues" include: perfect white teeth, bloodshot eyes, dirty fingernails, hairless armpits, etc. The sun is causing extreme heat, making it unsafe to go out during the day. Most visitors are hostile, and if the player lets in two or more vistors, they will be begin to kill any humans present in the house, eventually killing the owner.


The Backstory

Little is known about the visitors themselves. They seem to have been previously human, and are stated to have originally been buried, causing many to have dirty fingernails from diiging themselves out of the ground. A majority of visitors are not aware that they are visitors, those who are aware of their nature will be violient and threaten humans for fun.
